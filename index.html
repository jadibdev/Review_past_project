Steve Jobs

The eccentric innovator who brought us the iPhone

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, CA
  • 1972 - Attends Reed College
  • 1974 - Drops out and travels to India
  • 1976 - Founded Apple
  • 1977 - Releases the Apple II
  • 1983 - Releases the Apple Lisa
  • 1984 - Releases the Apple Machintosh
  • 1985 - Gets Fired from Apple
  • 1985 - Founded Next
  • 1986 - Takes over Pixar
  • 1995 - Produces Toy Story
  • 1996 - Apple buys Next for $427 million dollars and merges with it
  • 1997 - Back as CEO of Apple
  • 2001 - Releases the iPod
  • 2007 - Releases the iPhone
  • 2010 - releases the iPad
  • 2011 - Dies at the age of 56 in Palo Alto, CA
